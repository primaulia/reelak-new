About us

We bring films outside and into everyday spaces.

We believe great movies are meant to be enjoyed together in a communal setting. Each screening takes place in an open-air setting, at different locations aligned with the theme/genre of the film itself.

About our films

We want to screen films that are beautiful & thought-provoking, yet accessible.

Our films come from the rich and diverse catalog of international cinema, films that are hugely celebrated in their native soils, but didnʼt necessarily ʻmake itʼ in Hollywood. Through diverse works that tell meaningful stories in creative ways, we fill the space in between commercial and arthouse.
